About

We are a group of students with a dream and a drive. We want to make the world a better place for the next generation and we are doing that by improving the quality of life for the children of today.

Mission

Do you know how many children are overweight by the time they are five? How about by the time they are 11? The answers are respectively 1/5 and 1/3. This comes with some serious health complications for some very young people. A problem like this needs to be solved, so why not us?

App

Our app is the solution that we propose. It is an innovative way to get children in primary school to do exercise and have fun while they are doing it. We do this by using psychological techniques that engage them in order to
make it more
enjoyable.

How

We plan on tackling the problem of obesity by entering our application in the Appsforgood competition it is a real first world problem and we think it must be dealt with in the most effective way possible. That is by letting as many
people know about< our app.
